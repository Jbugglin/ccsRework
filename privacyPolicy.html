Privacy policy:

Although legal requirements may vary from country to country, CCS Mechanical Services intends to adhere to the principles set forth in this Online Privacy Policy even if, in connection with the above, we transfer your personal information from your country to countries that may not require an “adequate” level of protection for your Personal Information.
